Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

PuhuStudios ("we", "our", or "us") provides mobile games including Blockigo (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our App, how it is used, and the choices you have.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require account registration and we do not ask you to provide your name, address, or other direct personal details to play. However, the App and our third-party service providers may automatically collect certain information, such as:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, language, region)
  • App usage data (basic interactions, crash logs, performance information)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA) only if permitted by your device settings

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App may display ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may collect and process information to show personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS): If we request permission to track (IDFA), you can choose to allow or deny. You can change this anytime via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

4. How We Use Information

Information described above may be used to:

  • Operate and improve the App (stability, bug fixes, performance)
  • Provide ads and measure their effectiveness
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud or abuse

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the App (for example, advertising partners such as Google AdMob), subject to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

8. Your Choices

You can control certain data collection and advertising features:

  • iOS Tracking permission: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Ad personalization controls may also be available in your device settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at puhustudios@gmail.com.
